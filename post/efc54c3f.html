pep8、pep257等确认质量100%
https://github.com/edx/edx-platform/wiki/Python-Guidelines

https://github.com/edx/edx-platform/wiki/i18n-Coding-Guidelines

http://edx.readthedocs.org/projects/userdocs/en/latest/testing/index.html
